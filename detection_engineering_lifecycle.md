# Detection Engineering Lifecycle

Detection engineering follows a structured process.

---

## Step 1: Threat Intelligence

Understand attacker techniques and behaviors.

---

## Step 2: Telemetry Mapping

Identify available telemetry sources.

---

## Step 3: Detection Hypothesis

Define suspicious behavior patterns.

---

## Step 4: Detection Development

Create detection queries.

---

## Step 5: Validation

Test detections using attack simulations.

---

## Step 6: Tuning

Reduce false positives.

---

## Step 7: Deployment

Deploy detection to SIEM or EDR platform.

---

## Step 8: Continuous Improvement

Regularly review and improve detections.
